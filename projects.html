Reconnect Theatre Film Projects

In collaboration with Minnie Crook and Reconnect Theatres, this project is an offering to schools and young people to develop their creative and collaborative skills by co-creating a short film with two lead artists over the course of several weeks within the school. During the process, young people are encouraged to explore what their role might be in the creation process; director, camera operator, actor, choreographer etc. The project results in a film screening for parents, guardians and the whole school.

Spaceman's Spacewalk

SPACEMAN'S SPACEWALK

SPACEMAN'S SPACEWALK is a walkabout performance inspired by Don't Look, Spaceman. In this walkabout performance, Spaceman performs his first spacewalk in a new atmosphere, with zero gravity. Watch him as he VERY SLOWLY traverses his new surroundings taking in all the interactions that come along with him.

SPACEMAN'S SPACEWALK is an eye catching and exciting change of pace for any festival or family day situation.
