About

Artist Bio

Dan's practice is based in dance and theatre making, utilising persona and popular iconography to contextualise meaning and mediate his own autobiography, opening up the micro to relate to the macro. He co-creates processes and performances for non-performers to become autonomous makers and thinkers, to step into their agency by owning their autobiography.
